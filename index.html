What's This All About?

Hey, new grad! This tool helps you take control of your money like a pro—even if you're starting from zero. We use FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early), a simple idea: save and invest early, let your money grow, and retire when you want!

Imagine you're 12: you earn allowance (salary), spend some (expenses), and save the rest in a piggy bank (investments) that magically grows over time. Here, you'll see how much to save, invest in stocks/bonds, and hit your goals—easy peasy!

The best part? Starting right after college means your money has decades to grow. Even small investments now can turn into huge amounts later!
